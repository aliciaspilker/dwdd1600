Inspirations

Asha Puthli

  • Puthli is an Indian jazz and disco singer
  • In 2009, her music was trantransmitted into space to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the moon landing which led to Puthli indentifying as a “space cadet.”
  • She's always been a strong influence in Raveena's life and this is noticeable in the album Asha's Awakening where Puthli is featured on the song "Asha's Kiss."
  • Both Raveena and Puthli share the outlook of outer space being a "realm of escape and fantasy."

M.I.A.

  • M.I.A. is a British-Sri Lankan rapper, record producer, and singer.
  • She inspired Raveena in her teenage years after Raveena watched a person that she felt represented a version of herself get into mainstream music.
  • Raveena especially admires how she has control over every part of her career.
